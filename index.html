Step 1

Download and install the CurseForge app from here

Step 2

Open the CurseForge app and make sure Minecraft is recognized as a game

Step 3

Click on Minecraft.


Then click on the "Browse Modpacks" tab

Step 4

Using the search bar, search for "another quality modpack 3".


Then click install on "Another Quality Modpack 3 - 1.18.1"

Step 5

While it's installing, click on the settings cog in the bottom left hand corner.


Then click on "Minecraft" under "Game Specific" and scroll down to "Java Settings".

Use the slider to set the "Allocated Memory" to something between 6000MB and 10000MB

  • 6000MB - If you have 8GB+ of ram
  • 8000MB - If you have 12GB+ of ram or a high end PC
  • 10000MB - If you don't care and have a really good PC

Step 6

Finally, you can go back to the Minecraft homepage in the CurseForge app and click play
