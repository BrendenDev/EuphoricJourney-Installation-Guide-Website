Step 1

Download and install the CurseForge app from here

Step 2

Open the CurseForge app and make sure Minecraft is recognized as a game

Step 3

Click on Minecraft
Then click on the "Browse Modpacks" tab

Step 4

Using the search bar, search for "euphoric curiosity"
Then click on "Euphoric Curiosity"

Then hit the "Versions" tab and scroll down to "Euphoric Curiosity-1.6.zip"
Hit the download button on the right hand side of it

Downloading any other version will not work

Step 5

While it's installing, click on the settings cog in the bottom left hand corner
Then click on Minecraft and scroll down to the slider

Use the slider to set the "Allocated Memory" to something between 6000MB and 10000MB

  • 4000MB - If you have less than 8GB of ram
  • 6000MB - If you have 8GB+ of ram
  • 8000MB - If you have 12GB+ of ram
  • 10000MB - If you have 16GB+ of ram

You can find your ram by going to task manager and clicking on the "Performance" tab

Step 6

Finally, you can go back to the Minecraft homepage in the CurseForge app and click play

Final Note

Strong Recomendation for Minecraft Settings

  • Turn on V-Sync
  • Render distance of 8, play around with it
  • Set "Max Shadow Distance" and "Simulation Distance" to the same as or below your render distance
